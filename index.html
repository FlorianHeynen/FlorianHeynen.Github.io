About me

I am a technical sound designer and audio programmer. I have recently completed an internship at Demute in Brussels as part of my Interactive Sound Design studies at DAE. I have been a hobbyist game developer for about 6 years, making small games and participating in game jams. Over the past few years, I have specialized in the audio aspects of games. Learning sound design and music has been the most fulfilling experience in my game development journey, so I decided to pursue it as a career. Thanks to personal projects and my education at DAE, I have gained experience in every facet of game development. My technical know-how and creative abilities position me as a valuable asset for any game development studio. I love working in a team and solving problems together. Constantly seeking ways to improve my sound design or to make workflows more efficient. I am open to work at either indie or AA(A) companies, my primary goal is to join a passionate team committed to creating great games.
