About me

I am a technical sound designer. I am currently doing an Internship at Demute for my interactive sound design studies at DAE. I have been a hobbyist game developer for about 6 years, making small games and participating in game jams. Over the last years I have been focusing on the audio aspect of games. Learning sound design and music has been the most fulfilling experience in my game development journey, so I decided to pursue it as a career.

Projects

Dynamic horizontal music system

I did a research project on adaptive game music. My goal was to make Game music follow a structure like linear music does and make it feel like one contious piece of music. the music exists out of a few sections (ABABCB) wich are also seperated into small segments (2bars) so we can transition between sections without crossfading.
